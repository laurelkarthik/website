Wifi Hotspot

Wifi Zones are the geographical areas that enable the users to logon to the Internet through their choice of devices (Mobiles, Tablets, Laptops etc..). This is achieved by integrating various technologies like the RADIUS, ISP, Wifi devices and many such needed hardware while the usability is attained through a seemless software solution.

Bandwidth Management

A Remote Authentication Dial-In User Service (RADIUS) server is the software solution that manages the needs of any ISP in catering the bandwidth needs of the customers. Our RADIUS is superior to others, as it has all the common features like Subscriber Management, Billing, Bandwidth Management and all this is on the cloud. One can access the service from anywhere on the globe at any point in time, forgetting the maintenance of the hardware.

Internet Billing

Billing is the ultimate tool managing the monetary transactions of the subscribers. Any Business needs to manage the money that is generated by the services that it caters to the clientele. Our Billing application manages and automates all these requirements by sending out SMS and mails to the users. A payment gateway is integrated enabling the users to pay their dues online. The best thing is the whole billing application is API based and can be integrated to any software that the customer is using already as a module.

Ad and Campaign Management

Ads are the definitive strategies to promote one’s business in this competitive times. Ads generate interest and curiosity in the products and generate money on the sides. This needs a meticulous management system for better results. Our Ad and Campaign Management enables the users to create campaigns with loads of options for managing the target audience based on their age, gender and other demographics specified, and displayed according to the choice of time and day. Attendance, Leave and Claim Management

Any organization is driven by the business idea of the heads and is run on the relentless efforts of the workforce Our WorkForce Management is a one stop solution for managing the attendance, leaves and claims, if any from the employees. It can be fully automated by integrating it to the most biometric systems that are available in the market. The automation of these will reduce a lot of work on the HR enabling them to concentrate on other tasks and negates the human errors from the system.

GPS & Vehicle Tracking System

Logistics involves much more than fuel costs and drivers. Global Positioning System (GPS) allows us to know the specific location of the vehicle at any given point in time. Various hardware components that integrate with the main circuit allows the user to have the information vehicle’s route, fuel usage, engine status, temperature of the container in case of perishable goods etc. This information can be used by the organization to take better decisions and enable cost cuttings.
